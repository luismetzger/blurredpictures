DistantSounds is now real

——December 3, 2013——

DistantSounds is a project to promote independet culture, music and art.

The idea of empower this initiative with the release of the site dsounds.es is to transform into a wolrldwide collective. Already we have collaborators in Chile, Argentina and Venezuela. In the look of keep on growing and spreading the word of independent voices. You all are welcome in DS..

Part of the work of this group of people is to interview bands, visual and plastic artists, movies and theater actors, directors and everyone who is envolved in the art process. We want to share how was their first steps as independent artists. The idea is that our readers get closer to their's experiences, finding between this people's lines reasons to keep on doing what the like and following their passions no matter what it is.

Our articles goes from news, free blogs, reviews, #lyricsquote and interviews to a daily musical recommendation with the chance of finding new music in the monthly archive. Discover!

Help us spread the independent sounds and sensations. Someone always says it in a song:

«... it could be made into a monster if we all pull together as a team» — Pink Floyd
